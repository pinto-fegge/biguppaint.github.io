About Us

Since our founding, BIGUP High Quality Paint has delivered vibrant, ultra-high-quality paints and coatings to our customers.
We carefully produce BIGUP High Quality Paint with our high technical production equipments and we have trained painters with skills
to bring out the beauty of your homes and offices.
We always develop a first-hand knowledge of our customers and their processes ensures a consistency not only in product,
but in a successful working relationship. Quality effects matters to us.
We've been in the same location since we started - 41 Limca Road. Old Road Opposite Crunches, Onitsha Anambra State.
Even though we reside in Anambra, we still serve our customers all over Nigeria. We have done projects in different locations in Nigeria
like Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt and more.

OUR MISSION

Very simple, the mission of BIGUP High Quality Paint is:

  • To be our customers best paint supplier.

We will get there by living our company's Quality Policy. We are committed to satisfying our customers by designing,
manufacturing, and servicing products that will exceed their expectations through innovation, partnership, and continuous improvement.
Don't just take us on our word - give us a call, meet our staff, and learn first-hand that BIGUP High Quality Paint
should be your high-quality specialty paint and coating partner!
